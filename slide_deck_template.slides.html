Preliminary Wrangling¶

This dataset includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area.

1. user_type¶

The number of trips of Subscriber users is more than the number of trips of Customer users. In which (less than 160,000 trips of the total) 90.53% of the trips taken by Subscriber type whereas (less than 20,000 trips of the total) 9.47% of the trips taken by the customer type.
